Sipo

Description:

A popular filipino dish that is mainly made with shrimp and vegetables,
this recipe only uses quail eggs as the primary source of protein and a
little bit of chicken as substitute to the shrimp.

Originated from Pampanga, this budget-friendly dish is simple to make
and delicious to pair with steamed rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbps margarine
  • 1 tbps oil
  • 1 chicken breast or half kilo shrimp
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 shrimp knorr cube (optional)
  • 1 chicken knorr cube
  • 1 canned corn
  • 5 tbps grated cheese
  • 20 quail eggs
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 1 250 ml all purpose cream

Steps:

  1. Boil quail eggs, peel once cooked, then set aside
  2. Heat pan with oil and margarine
  3. Saute carrot, corn, green peas one by one. Set aside each one
  4. Saute garlic and onions
  5. Prepare chicken, slice it in cubes then add in on pan
  6. Add shrimp if available. If shrimp is not available use shrimp knorr cube instead
  7. Add carrots, corn, and green peas on pan
  8. Add chicken cube, let cube dissolve before adding the cheese and egg. Be careful not to break eggs.
  9. Once all ingredients are mixed, cream of mushroom and all purpose cream then let it simmer for 20 mins
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
