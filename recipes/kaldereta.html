Chicken Kaldereta

Description:

Chicken Kaldereta is a variation from the original goat meat stew kaldereta.
Commonly this stew is made with goat meat, vegetable, and liver paste but,
in this recipe we will be using chicken as our meat and add coconut milk to
add a savory and creamy texture into our dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 carrots
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 white onions
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tbps margarine
  • 2 tbps oil
  • 2 tbps soy Sauce
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 chicken Knorr cubes or 500 ml chicken stock
  • 250g tomato sauce
  • 1 can liver spread
  • 1/2 stick of cheese
  • 500 ml coconut milk
  • 1 tbps italian seasoning (Optional)
  • 1 siling labuyo (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat stew pot with oil and margarine. Saute potatoes and carrots and set aside once brown.
  2. Saute vegetables (garlic, onions, bell pepper, and siling labuyo).
  3. Add soy sauce and stir till it complements to the vegetables.
  4. Fry chicken with the vegetables
  5. Add 1 Knorr cube with the potatoes and carrots
  6. Add Tomato sauce till simmer.
  7. Add another Knorr cube and cheese till melt
  8. Add coconut milk and let the stew boil
  9. Season with salt and pepper and italian seasoning.

    10. Note: add sugar to balance sourness of stew.
