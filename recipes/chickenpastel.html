Chicken Pastel:

Description:

A chicken stew that was originated from Spain's pastel de pollo during
their era, the philippines adapted the dish to create a rich creamy stew that
became a common filipino cuisine that is mainly made of diced chicken, sausage,
mushrooms and vegetables. This is a hearty dish that one should make!

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 hungarian sausage or chorizo
  • 2 chicken breast
  • 2 mushrooms
  • 4 laurels
  • 1 tbsp dried basil or italian seasoning
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potatoes
  • 370 ml evaporated milk
  • 1 chicken knorr cube or 450 ml chicken stock
  • 1/2 stick of cheese
  • 250 ml all purpose cream
  • 100 ml fish sauce or 1 pack of magic sarap
  • 2 calamansi or 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper or soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with fish sauce or magic sarap, calamansi or lemon, salt & pepper or soy sauce for 30 min-1 hour.
  2. Fry potatoes & carrots with oil & butter on pan medium heat. Set aside once vegetables are soft
  3. Saute sausage or chorizo on medium heat till brown. Once done set aside.
  4. Saute garlic, onion, and bell peppers till caramelized
  5. Add chicken and italian seasoning (optional). Let it until ingredients are well mixed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper or magic sarap.
  7. Add evaporated milk and stir.
  8. Set stove to low heat, cover until cream boils to thicken the sauce.
  9. Add potatoes and carrots with all purpose cream and cheese. Stir and let it cook for 5 minutes.
  10. Season with salt and pepper until desired taste.
