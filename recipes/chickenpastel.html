Chicken Pastel

Description

A chicken stew that was originated from Spain's pastel de pollo during
their era, the philippines adapted the dish to create a rich creamy stew that
became a common filipino cuisine that is mainly made of diced chicken, sausage,
mushrooms and vegetables. This is a hearty dish that one should make!

Ingredients

  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 hungarian sausage or chorizo
  • 2 chicken breast
  • 2 mushrooms
  • 4 laurels
  • 1 tbsp dried basil or italian seasoning
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potatoes
  • 370 ml evaporated milk
  • 1 chicken knorr cube or 450 ml chicken stock
  • 1/2 stick of cheese
  • 250 ml all purpose cream
